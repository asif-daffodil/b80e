Daffodil International University





Daffodil International University is a private university located in Dhaka, Bangladesh. Here are the admission details for undergraduate programs: Eligibility Criteria: Applicants must have completed their Higher Secondary Certificate (HSC) or equivalent examination. Applicants must have a minimum GPA of 2.5 (out of 5.0) in both SSC and HSC or equivalent examinations. For the Science and Engineering programs, applicants must have a minimum GPA of 2.5 in both SSC and HSC or equivalent examinations with a minimum GPA of 2.5 in Mathematics and Physics at the HSC or equivalent level. For the Bachelor of Architecture program, applicants must have a minimum GPA of 2.5 in both SSC and HSC or equivalent examinations with a minimum GPA of 2.5 in Mathematics and Physics at the HSC or equivalent level. Application Process: Applicants can apply online through the university's admission portal (https://admission.daffodilvarsity.edu.bd/). The application fee is 1,200 BDT. Applicants must provide all the necessary information and upload the required documents, such as academic transcripts and certificates, a passport-sized photograph, and a national ID card or passport. Admission Test: Applicants who meet the eligibility criteria are required to sit for an admission test. The admission test comprises multiple-choice questions from English, Mathematics, General Knowledge, and Analytical Ability. The duration of the test is 90 minutes, and the total marks are 100. Admission Result: The admission result will be published on the university's website and notice board. Selected candidates will be notified via email and SMS. Selected candidates must confirm their admission by paying the admission fee within the stipulated time. Note: The admission process may vary for postgraduate programs. For detailed information, please visit the university's website or contact the admission office.
